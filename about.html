From VP of Finance to AI Builder

I spent years in accounting and finance — working as a consultant, climbing to VP of Finance, learning how businesses actually make and lose money. The one thing every small business owner told me? "I can't afford to miss calls, but I can't afford a receptionist either."

When AI voice technology matured enough to actually sound human, I saw the opportunity. I didn't hire a team or raise funding. I sat down, taught myself the tools — Vapi, OpenAI, Twilio — and built a working AI receptionist for a real hair salon. Then a bridal shop. Then a plumbing company.

Three live demos later, AI SB Consulting was born. Not from a pitch deck — from working systems you can call right now.
