From VP of Finance to AI Builder

Andrew Curtis is the founder of AISB Consulting, a Boston-based technology consultancy that helps small businesses and startups never miss a customer call. He builds and deploys AI-powered voice reception systems that automatically handle appointment and service calls, integrate with calendars and customer databases, and deliver a seamless experience at an affordable price.

Drawing on years of experience solving complex workflow problems, Andrew created a turnkey solution specifically for SMBs that need reliable automation without enterprise-level complexity or cost. As a full-time founder, he's passionate about giving small business owners powerful technology that saves them time and grows their business.
