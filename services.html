How the Audit Works

Four phases. Start to finish in under a week. You walk away knowing exactly where AI fits your business — and what to do first.

1

The Interview

A 45-minute deep dive into how you actually run your business — your calendar, your inbox, your workflows, your pain points. No prep needed. We ask the questions. You just talk.

2

The AI Analysis

We run your interview through an AI analysis that identifies 3–7 high-impact opportunities specific to your business. Then we review every finding ourselves — cutting anything that's a stretch, adding recommendations from our own toolkit.

3

The Report

You receive a clear, professional written report — delivered within 24 hours. It includes:

  • Executive summary with your top 3 opportunities
  • Priority matrix — high impact + low effort = start here
  • Tool recommendations with implementation details
  • 4-day quick start plan so you know exactly what to do first
  • Financial impact breakdown — real numbers, not estimates
4

The Review Call

A 30-minute call to walk through the report together, answer your questions, and make sure you leave with a real plan — not just a document. You'll know what to do, in what order, and why it's worth it.
