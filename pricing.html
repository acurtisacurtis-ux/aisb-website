Common Pricing Questions

Are there any setup fees?

No setup fees for AI Voice Receptionist plans. Bookkeeping onboarding includes a one-time setup that covers system configuration, chart of accounts, and initial workflow builds.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What accounting platforms do you support?

We work with Zoho Books (recommended for new clients), QuickBooks Online, and Xero. No forced migrations — we work with what you already use.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
